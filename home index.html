About us

Nestled in the heart of Own farms, Andhra Pradesh, Suhanveer Exim carries forward a rich heritage of horticultural excellence. Our plants are renowned for its thriving nurseries and high-quality plants, and our company is proud to say that we have 200+ plants. With generations of growers perfecting the craft, we deliver true heritage plants rooted in tradition.
